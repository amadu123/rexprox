Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I keep getting errors?

The most likely reason is that you are entering a subdirectory that doesn't exist or it was banned/in development. Ex: astrox-web.glitch.me/life


Why is a site marked as deceptive?

Google will sometimes mistake a traditional site for a scam site. The best thing you can do in this situation is to report it as a mistake here


Why is it when I click on a button, nothing happens?

This is an issue with your browsers website viewer. Just press CTRL + Shift + R, and click on the button again. If this doesn't work, Please restart your browser and try again later.
